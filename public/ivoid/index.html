iVOID.hosts

iVOID.hosts is dedicated to providing AdGuard Pro for iOS users with a custom built, privacy & security focused hosts file. Included are active phishing hosts from Hardenize.com.

Their novel method involves the monitoring of Certificate Transparency for the acquisition of the active phishing domains that iVOID.hosts includes in its hosts file, thanks to the generous licensing that Hardenize has granted us.

iVOID.hosts is also for use with Linux workstations. It's also been tested and works well with FreeBSD, OpenBSD, & NetBSD; macOS; & Windows 7, 8.x and 10.x as a hosts file for /etc/hosts (or the equvilent relative to the OS in use). Moreover, it works well with uBlock Origin or AdGuard for Firefox.
