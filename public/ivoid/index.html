iVOID.hosts


iVOID.hosts is dedicated to providing AdGuard Pro users with a with a filter built from active phishing hosts licensed from Hardenize.com.

Their novel method of detection involves the monitoring of Certificate Transparency for the acquisition of the active phishing domains that iVOID.hosts is built from, thanks to the generous licensing that Hardenize has granted us.

iVOID.hosts is also for use with Linux workstations. It's also been tested and works well with BSD & macOS as a hosts file for /etc/hosts (or the equivalent relative to the OS in use). Moreover, it works well with uBlock Origin & AdGuard for Firefox.
